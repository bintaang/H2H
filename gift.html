For you, the part of the story

In midnight echoes, your soft voice drifts— like moonlight dancing on quiet waves. A ripple of warmth, a tender sigh, you whisper “hello” through heart‑shaped haze.

Your words, they float on dusty rose air, each syllable a petal falling slow. The world tilts into your private glow where echoes hum a secret only we know.

When laughter blooms behind pink‑tinted doors, I tiptoe in, heart drumming hope and ease. You shimmer courage into fragile chords, and weave small gifts of starlight on the breeze.

So let me build this little haven here— a cozy nook where gentle truths can grow. With every door you pick, you gift your soul; in every pause, our shared soft afterglow.

From the bottom of my heart, Reza.
